## 灵感音乐从哪里来

灵感音乐一开始不是为了做一个“功能很多但很冷”的播放器。它更像一个小小的音乐房间：你的专辑、播放列表、歌词和最近播放会被安静地收好；当你不知道听什么的时候，它也能根据一句话、一种心情或一个场景，递给你一份新的歌单灵感。

所以它叫“灵感音乐”。它不只负责播放，也负责在某个晚上、某段路上、某个突然想听歌的瞬间，帮你把音乐重新点亮。🎧

## 你会看到什么

- 🫧 Liquid Glass 风格的底栏、mini 播放器、按钮、卡片和设置界面。
- 🎵 本地音乐资料库：歌曲、专辑、艺人、播放列表和我的喜爱。
- ✨ AI 灵感歌单：用自然语言描述想听的音乐，API 服务由你自己配置。
- 📝 音乐日记：按日记、周记、月记回看自己的听歌痕迹。
- 🎤 本地歌词、内嵌歌词、在线歌词搜索和歌词缓存管理。
- 💾 播放列表、音乐日记和最近播放数据备份。
- 📦 音乐存储空间统计。
- 📱 横屏播放布局和沉浸式桌面小组件。

## AI 与隐私

播放器、资料库、歌词缓存、音乐日记和备份功能都在本机运行。AI 推荐是可选功能，公开版本不会内置个人 API Key；使用 AI 时，请在设置中配置你自己的服务商、地址、模型和密钥。

在线歌词、元数据和 AI 功能可能会向对应服务发送请求。关闭在线功能后，灵感音乐仍然可以作为本地音乐播放器使用。

# Inspire Music

Inspire Music is not trying to turn your music into a cold spreadsheet. It is meant to feel more like a small listening room: your albums, playlists, lyrics, and recent plays stay quietly organized, while the Inspire page can help when you only have a mood, a scene, or one vague sentence in your head.

## Highlights

- 🫧 Liquid Glass-inspired bottom bar, mini player, buttons, cards, and settings.
- 🎵 Local songs, albums, artists, playlists, and favorites.
- ✨ AI inspiration playlists with user-configured providers and API keys.
- 📝 Music Diary with daily, weekly, and monthly listening memories.
- 🎤 Local, embedded, online, and cached lyrics.
- 💾 Backups for playlists, Music Diary, and recently played data.
- 📦 Music storage usage overview.
- 📱 Landscape playback and an immersive home-screen widget.

## Download

Download the latest signed APK from the [public Releases repository](https://github.com/Kwanlam08/InspireMusic-Releases/releases).

## AI And Privacy

Core playback, the library, lyric cache, Music Diary, and backups run locally. AI recommendations are optional and public builds do not contain a personal API key. Configure your own provider in Settings and follow its terms of service.
